French Press

Description

French press coffee is a delicious alternative to the common drip method. It provides the coffee with a comparatively more developed flavor profile.

Not only do you get a tastier cup, it also requires no filter to be replaced each time.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Coarse Grind Coffee

Steps

  1. Begin boiling 500ml of water.
  2. Add 3-5 tablespoons of coffee to the press, depending on desired strength.
  3. Pour the boiling water all into the press.
  4. Close the lid of the press and wait 5 minutes.
  5. Open the lid and stir the floating grounds back into the coffee.
  6. Close the lid and wait 3 minutes.
  7. Lower the plunger down to the level of the coffee.
  8. Pour into your cup and enjoy!
