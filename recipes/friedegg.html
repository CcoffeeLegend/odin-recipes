Fried Egg

Description

Fried eggs are a quick and easy solution for a healthy and filling meal.

Considering every egg contains around 6 grams of protein, they make a phenomenal addition to the diet of fitness enthusiasts.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Olive Oil or Butter

Steps

  1. Add just enough oil or butter to a nonstick pan to cover the surface.
  2. Heat the pan up to a medium level of heat.
  3. Crack an egg open and add to the pan.
  4. Allow the egg to cook for around one minute.
  5. Carefully flip the egg to cook the other side for one minute.
  6. Remove from the pan, and enjoy!
